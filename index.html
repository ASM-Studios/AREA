AREA documentation

Welcome to the AREA documentation!

AREA (Action Reaction) aims to connect multiple services together, similar to IFTTT. With AREA, you can create automated workflows that integrate various services and perform actions based on specific triggers.

Features

  • Connect Multiple Services: Integrate with a wide range of services to automate your workflows.

  • Customizable Workflows: Create custom workflows tailored to your needs.

  • User-Friendly Interface: Easy-to-use interface for setting up and managing your workflows.
